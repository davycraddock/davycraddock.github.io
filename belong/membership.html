Membership Details

Our Club

Cronulla Triathlon Club is a supportive, family orientated community of like-minded people. We cater for all abilities from beginners through to experienced athletes. We are passionate about creating an environment that supports our motto of SUPPORT, FUN, SAFE and ACTIVE. Membership with Cronulla Triathlon Club gives you access to a wide variety of training sessions in a supportive and competitive environment. As a community, our members come together to support one another with most of the training arranged outside of our regular sessions via our private social channel. You also get exclusive offers and discounts from our local sponsors. Interested in joining or a new member? Reach out to our Memberships Officer by e-mail at belong@cronullatriclub.com.au We can answer your questions or help you to get started.

Membership Options

● Training only (basic package) membership – includes access to all club training sessions, but not include insurance or licenses to race.

● Competing (standard or premium package) membership – includes access to all club training sessions and does include insurance and licenses to race.

● Social membership, if training and racing is not for you this season. As a social member you get all the benefits of the being part of the club including exclusive sponsor offers, but does not include insurance for training and racing.

Trial Memberships

Cronulla Triathlon Club also offers trial members, which allows you to train with our club, including a free 14-day trial, or an 8-week TRY BEFORE YOU BUY program for $25. Both options provide you with access all club training sessions during the membership period. Contact belong@cronullatriclub.com.au if you have any questions.

Active Kids Vouchers

Cronulla Triathlon Club is an approved Active Kids provider! Your Active Kids voucher can be used for the Cronulla Triathlon Club Active Kids Membership Package which includes:

● Junior CTC Membership (with insurance & training access)

● Club membership pack (t-shirt & swim cap)

● Entry into FIVE club races (aquathlons, duathlons, triathlons - excluding Richie Walker Aquathlon)

Apply for your Active Kids Voucher with Service NSW.

To use the voucher, select an Active Kids Membership Package when registering for the season. Juniors over 16 years have the option of a Premium or Standard package, while those under 16 will have only the Standard package option available if using your Active Kids voucher. Once you have entered your Active Kids voucher code into the provided field, the total price to pay at the checkout will be the cost of the Triathlon Australia / TriNSW fee only.

How to join Cronulla Triathlon Club

To join our club, you must first be a member of Triathlon Australia and Triathlon NSW, as our sport has a compulsory membership model in which all racing club members are required to be members of their relevant State Association. The benefits of joining TA and TriNSW include discounted race entry and third-party insurance for training and racing.

Simply register with the Triathlon Australia membership portal following the link below. Choose Cronulla Triathlon Club as your club and will see a range of membership options available to you. Our membership fees are listed below.
