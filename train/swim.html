F.A.S.T. Friday Afternoon Swim Time

Join us for our weekly club swim. We aim to provide a fun and inclusive environment for all swimmers.

What to Expect

We usually meet at the Gunnamatta Pavilion, before heading in to the water. We swim altogether stopping every 400m to regroup. Its a casual swim not a race and a perfect way to start the weekend.

Time

In the water for 4:30pm - wraps up around 5:30pm

Additional information

Depending on the weather and sea conditions, we may adjust the swim location. Please check our website or WhatsApp group for updates.
