Wednesday Club Run

On every Wednesday hail or shine this is a club staple. All levels welcome, we separate out into groups depending on ability and your welcome to choose your own pace/distance.

What to Expect

Arrive at the venue 10 minutes before the start time to get warmed up and hear the latest club news. Stay afterwards for a catch-up with fellow members and enjoy a beverage.

Time

Starts 6:20pm - wraps up around 7:30pm

Contact

For additional information, reach out to train@cronullatriclub.com.au. or for club members use our WhatsApp group.
