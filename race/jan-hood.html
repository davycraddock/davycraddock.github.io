Monday Adult Bike Skills

Join us for an exhilarating bike skills session every Monday at 6.30pm at the Waratah Bike Track, located at Waratah Park, Rawson Avenue, Sutherland. Whether you’re a beginner eager to boost your confidence or an advanced rider aiming to refine your skills, this session is tailored for all levels.

What to Expect

Gain confidence and enhance your biking skills under the guidance of experienced instructors. Don’t forget to come prepared in your cycling gear and helmet for a safe and enjoyable experience. Stay hydrated by bringing along your favourite drink and ensure visibility with front and rear lights.

Time

Starts 6:30pm - wraps up around 7:30pm

Contact

For additional information, reach out to train@cronullatriclub.com.au. or for club members use our WhatsApp group.
