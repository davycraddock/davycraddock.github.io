Club Champion

The athelete who obtains the most points from the alloted races will become this seasons club champion.

Point Scorer Results

The results will be available to download here throughout the season once it starts.

Point Scorer Races and Conditions

● In order to earn points you must be a financial member at the time of each race.

●To be eligible for the end of year awards you must have completed a minimum of two designated races and participated or volunteered in at least one club race (Triathlon or Duathlon, excluding the Richie Walker Aquathlon) during the season.

● To obtain Race Points at Club Champs in February 2026 you must be racing for Cronulla Triathlon Club.

● Club Champs is a Double Point Score Race.
