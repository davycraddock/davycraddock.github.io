Event Details

Race Hard. Celebrate Harder!

It’s that time of year again with Club Championships in Forster coming up in February. We want YOU on the start line so CTC can go back to back! Whether you’re chasing points, chasing a PB, or just chasing an epic weekend away with the best tri community in Australia… this is the event you don’t want to miss.

The Plan (AKA: The Best Weekend of the Year)

Team HQ – Lanis Holiday Island, Forster Once again, we’re taking over Lanis! Think cabins full of teammates, pre-race banter, post-race laughs, and that unbeatable team atmosphere.

Friday Night Pre-Race Dinner

We kick things off with a relaxed Friday night dinner at Forster Bowling Club where the whole club gets together. Great food. Good vibes. Final race tips. Zero stress. Perfect way to settle in and get race-ready.

Saturday Morning - Race Time!

This is Club Champs - where every athlete counts.

Standard, Standard Aquabike, Sprint, Sprint Aquabike, Super Sprint, TriKidz, Teams. Whatever your distance, your effort and volunteering earns points for the club.

Bring your energy and your Cronulla colours.

Let’s make that blue & white impossible to miss on course.

Post-Race Party - Lani’s Holiday Island

After the finish line celebrations, we roll back to Lani’s for:

✔ Pizza

✔ Drinks

✔ Full club debrief (featuring dramatic retellings of transition mishaps, heroic sprint finishes & questionable nutrition choices).

This is the heart of the weekend — celebrating every athlete’s effort.

Presentation Night - Club Spirit in Full Force

Then we jump on the party bus and head to the official presentation night.

Dress Code: feeling proud

Vibe: electric

Goal: Bring home that top spot!
